About

There are many benefits to motivational quotes. These include putting people in the right frame of mind, helping them become more optimistic and more driven. Motivational quotes have been passed down generations for centuries and new ones are continuously being added to that list. Most common motivational quotes are spoken by historical figures which serve as inspiration for others

This app is used to study the importance of tailored quotes to help motivation. Users can generate a quote based on a keyword relating to their goal. As this is used for a study the application will not hold a large amount of quotes at the moment.
