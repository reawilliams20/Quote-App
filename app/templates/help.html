Help

To generate a quote go to the homepage, enter in your goal and a keyword relating to your goal, then click on one of the AI (Artificial Intelligence) buttons at the bottom of the form. Participants in the study are required to use this application twice a day (for each AI) for 5 days. For example you may choose to use AI 1 in the morning then AI 2 in the afternoon. But you must not select the same AI twice.

Once your quote has been generated set a timer for 20 minutes (DO NOT CLOSE THE PAGE, keep the tab open whilst completing other tasks). Once the 20 minutes are up navigate back to the app and click on the "Go to Questionnaire" button at the bottom of the page and answer the questions in the questionnaire. Users also have the option to set an alert which would notify you every 10 minutes on your goal and quote. You also have the option to generate another quote if you're not satisfied with the current one.
